The Team

Who We Are

We are a student led project team that designs and builds pedestrian footbridges for rurally isolated communities.
Our team of 26 passionate students works together to build a safe and innovative bridge for the betterment of isolated communities.

Cultural Team

The cultural subteam is responsible for developing lessons for the entire team to better understand the culture of the country
that we will be building at the following summer. They hold biweekly workshops and expose the team to the
country's language and cultural activities
